###  Masse-fjeder system
![[Pasted image 20251111123647.png]]
Hvis man skal laplacetransformerer en simpel fjeder:
$$
\begin{align*}
m \ddot{x} &=  f_{net}\\
f_{net} &= -kx \\
m \ddot{x} &= - kx\\
\ddot{x} &= - \frac{k}{m} x + u\\
\text{Laplace transformeret:}\\
\left(s^{2} + \frac{k}{m}\right) X(s) &= u(s)\\
X(s)/ (m s^{2} + k) &= F(s)\\
\frac{X(s)}{F(s)} &= \frac{1}{m s^{2} + k}\\
&= \frac{1}{s^{2} + \frac{k}{m}}
\end{align*}
$$
