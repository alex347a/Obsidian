## KÃ¦deregel
$$
\frac{d}{dx}[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x)
$$
## Kvotientregel
$$
\frac{d}{dx} \left[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right] = \frac{f'(x) \cdot g(x) - f(x) \cdot g'(x)}{g(x)^2}
$$

## Example:

$$
f(x) = \frac{x^2}{x + 1}
$$

1. $u(x) = x^2$, so $u'(x) = 2x$.
2. $v(x) = x + 1$, so $v'(x) = 1$.

$$
f'(x) = \frac{(2x)(x + 1) - (x^2)(1)}{(x + 1)^2}
$$

Simplify:

$$
f'(x) = \frac{2x^2 + 2x - x^2}{(x + 1)^2} = \frac{x^2 + 2x}{(x + 1)^2}.
$$
