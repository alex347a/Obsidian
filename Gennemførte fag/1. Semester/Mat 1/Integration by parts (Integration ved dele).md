$$
\begin{align*}\\
\text{Integration by parts formula:} \quad \int u \, dv &=  uv - \int v \, du\\
\text{Where:}\\
u & = \text{a function of $x$ to differentiate,} \\
dv & = \text{a function of $x$ to integrate.}
\end{align*}

$$
Eksempel med $ln(x)$:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Integration af } \ln(x): \\
\int \ln(x) \, dx &= \int u \, dv \quad \text{(integration ved dele, hvor } u = \ln(x) \text{ og } dv = dx). \\
\text{SÃ¥:} \quad du &= \frac{1}{x} \, dx, \quad v = x. \\
\int \ln(x) \, dx &= x \ln(x) - \int x \cdot \frac{1}{x} \, dx \\
&= x \ln(x) - \int 1 \, dx \\
&= x \ln(x) - x + C.
\end{align*}
