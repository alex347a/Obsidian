$$

Serie:  
De øverste er begge 2 10:  
$$
\frac{10}{2} = 5 \, \Omega 
$$

$$
27,27 + 5 = 32,27 \, \Omega 
$$

$$
\frac{1}{32,27 \, \Omega} + \frac{1}{20 \, \Omega} = \frac{52,27}{645,4} = 12,35 \, \Omega 
$$
