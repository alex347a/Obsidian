$$
x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}
$$

$$
z = f(x,y) \quad \text{ eller } \quad g(x,y,z) = 0
$$

z = \pm \sqrt{a^{2} - x^{2} - y^{2}} \quad \text{ eller } \quad g(x,y,z) =  x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} - a^{2} = 0
