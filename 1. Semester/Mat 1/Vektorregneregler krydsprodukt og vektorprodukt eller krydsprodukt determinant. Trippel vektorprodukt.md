$$\text{Addition af vektorer:} \quad \vec{a} + \vec{b} = (a_x + b_x) \hat{\imath} + (a_y + b_y) \hat{\jmath} + (a_z + b_z) \hat{k}$$

$$\text{Subtraktion af vektorer:} \quad \vec{a} - \vec{b} = (a_x - b_x) \hat{\imath} + (a_y - b_y) \hat{\jmath} + (a_z - b_z) \hat{k}$$

$$\text{Skalar multiplikation:} \quad c \cdot \vec{a} = c \cdot a_x \hat{\imath} + c \cdot a_y \hat{\jmath} + c \cdot a_z \hat{k}$$

$$\text{Prikprodukt (dot product):} \quad \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_x b_x + a_y b_y + a_z b_z$$

$$\text{Krydsprodukt (cross product):} \quad \vec{a} \times \vec{b} = 
\begin{vmatrix}
\hat{\imath} & \hat{\jmath} & \hat{k} \\
a_x & a_y & a_z \\
b_x & b_y & b_z
\end{vmatrix}$$
